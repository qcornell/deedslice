What Is DeedSlice?

DeedSlice is real estate tokenization infrastructure. Property operators use it to tokenize properties, manage investors, and distribute returns — all from one dashboard.

  • 🏠 Tokenize any property in under 5 minutes — NFT deed + share tokens on Hedera blockchain
  • 👥 Investor portal — investors sign up, see their holdings, track distributions
  • 💰 Distribution management — auto-calculate splits, notify investors, record on-chain
  • 🔒 Tamper-proof audit trail — every event recorded on Hedera Consensus Service
  • 🏷️ White-label ready — operators deploy under their own brand and custom domain
  • 📄 PDF certificates, document vault, compliance tools

Competitors charge $40,000+ for this. DeedSlice is $299/month.
