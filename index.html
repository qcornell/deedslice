How It Works

Three steps to tokenized property.

From property details to on-chain assets in under five minutes.

1

Describe

Enter property details — name, valuation, number of slices. DeedSlice handles the on-chain structure automatically.

2

Structure

DeedSlice auto-creates your on-chain bundle: NFT master deed, fungible share tokens, and an HCS audit trail — all linked.

3

Deploy

One click deploys everything to Hedera. Your investor dashboard goes live — share ownership, transfer slices, audit every action.

Capabilities

Everything you need to structure
property as a digital asset.

📜

NFT Master Deeds

Each property gets a unique NFT on Hedera Token Service — the immutable on-chain record serving as the digital deed.

🪙

Fractional Share Tokens

HTS fungible tokens represent ownership slices. Configurable supply, decimals, and transfer controls.

📋

HCS Audit Trails

Every action logged to Hedera Consensus Service — document hashes, transfers, valuations. Tamper-proof forever.

🔐

Investor Verification

Built-in KYC and accreditation checks. Verify investor identity before token distribution — compliance-ready from day one.

📊

Investor Dashboard

Clean dashboard for token holders. Ownership distribution, transfer history, document vault, valuation tracking.

⚡

REST API & Webhooks

Programmatic access to every feature. Webhooks for transfers, valuations, and audit entries. Build on top of DeedSlice.
